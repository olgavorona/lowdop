Purchase Help

  • All purchases are processed by Apple through your App Store account
  • If a purchase does not appear right away, reopen the app and try “Restore Purchases”
  • You can manage active subscriptions in your device’s App Store subscription settings

Restore Purchases

If you already bought access and it is not unlocked, open the app and use the Restore Purchases button from the paywall or account area.
